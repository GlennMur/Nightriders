Nightrider 'Versus' Leaderboard

 

This is in trial in 2021

 

Concept of additional leaderboard where your team compete against all other teams each game.

Rules:

 

·         Game 1

o   Bowler 1 score + Hdcp versus all other Bowler 1's score + hdcp – winner 2 points

o   Bowler 2 score + Hdcp versus all other Bowler 2's score + hdcp – winner 2 points

o   Team score + Hdcp versus all other Team's score + hdcp – winner 4 points

·         Game 2

o   Bowler 1 score + Hdcp versus all other Bowler 1's score + hdcp – winner 2 points

o   Bowler 2 score + Hdcp versus all other Bowler 2's score + hdcp – winner 2 points

o   Team score + Hdcp versus all other Team's score + hdcp – winner 4 points

·         Game 3

o   Bowler 1 score + Hdcp versus all other Bowler 1's score + hdcp – winner 2 points

o   Bowler 2 score + Hdcp versus all other Bowler 2's score + hdcp – winner 2 points

o   Team score + Hdcp versus all other Team's score + hdcp – winner 4 points

·         Overall

o   Team 3-game score + Hdcp versus all other Team's 3-game score + hdcp - winner 4 points

 

Summary

 

·         Game 1 - 8 points in total to be won

·         Game 2 - 8 points in total to be won

·         Game 3 - 8 points in total to be won

·         Overall - 4 points in total to be won

·         Total - 28 Points each week

 

In the event of any tie the points will be evenly shared
